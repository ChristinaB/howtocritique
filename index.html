HOW TO CRITIQUE

Good critique is an integral part of a productive design process. Through feedback and constructive dialogue it can help a designer to refine and develop their work beyond what they might accomplish on their own. This site contains resources created to assist in the process of critique – we hope they’re helpful.

Giving and receiving feedback works best when those involved use a common language. The “SHARED WORDS” present themes and definitions that are important for participants to understand together before engaging in a critique.
